Keenan Allen

Keenan Allen is an American football player who is a wide receiver currently for the Los Angeles Chargers. Growing up in Greensboro, North Carolina, Allen eventually started playing for the Golden Bears at the University of California, Berkeley. Eventually, he left his junior year where the Chargers selected him as a 12th pick during the 2013 NFL draft. In 2017 he was awarded multiple rookie honors and was named NFL Comeback Player of the Year due to consistent receiving yards and receptions.
